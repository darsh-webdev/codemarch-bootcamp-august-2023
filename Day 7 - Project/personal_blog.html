Cultivating a Reading Habit

Posted on August 7, 2023 by Darshan Rajput

Since the start of this year, I decided to develop healthy habits in myself and I choose Reading as one of them! In this post, I'd like to share some highlights and tips on how you can develop a reading habit too.

Since I've started, reading has transformed my life in an insane number of ways, and I couldn’t be more thankful that it’s a thing I’ve made a habit of doing. It has helped me to reduce stress, improve my focus and concentration, get better sleep and considerably increase my vocabulary.

Here are 5 tips for how you can develop a reading habit at home:

  • Make a List: Keep a list of all the great books to want to read.
  • Make it pleasureable: Read books that you highly enjoy or it will feel like a chore.
  • Set times: Have a few set times throughout the day when you'll read for atleast 5-10 minutes. If you even start with 3 times a day you'll read for 30 minutes a day.
  • Find a quite place: find a place at your home where you can sit comfortably and with any interruptions.
  • Set a goal: For example, you could set a goal to read a certain number of books this year or a certain number of pages each day and then work on reaching that goal. This will motivate you to read more.
